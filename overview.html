Overview

Identity And Mission

Peace of Mind is a mental health website that strives to educate everyone about the types of mental conditions that exist and how to combat them. It also advocates for prevention, recognition of symptoms, and treatment for anyone affected by mental illnesses. We envision a future where everyone is healthy and happy!
